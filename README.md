## What Is LiftLab?

LiftLab is the no-nonsense calculator suite for lifters who track their numbers. Not a social network. Not a subscription app. Just **10 precision tools** that answer the questions every serious gym-goer needs answered:

- *"What's my one-rep max based on today's lifts?"*
- *"How many calories and grams of protein do I actually need to bulk?"*
- *"What body fat percentage am I at?"*
- *"How long until I recover from this training block?"*

No fluff. No upsells inside the tools. Just clean, instant, accurate answers — and a conversion opportunity waiting right after every result.
